Survey Instruction

Please read the following instruction carefully.

On your desk, you'll find a folder containing a $5 coupon. During the upcoming survey, you'll be presented with a randomly selected mobile app and asked whether you currently use it. If yes, you'll be asked to evaluate 5 shortlisted features for the app's upcoming update. Choose the feature you would most like to use and specify how much you'd pay for using it using the $5 coupon (payment options range from 0 to 5).

Upon completing the survey and exiting the room, a staff member will redeem the coupon. Among all the products you assessed, one already has a beta version. We will provide you with credentials to access the features you selected in the survey. Your indicated payment will be deducted from the coupon. The staff will hand you the remaining amount in cash.
